Tour of Paris :

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world. It was built in 1889 for the World's Fair, and it has been a popular tourist destination ever since.

Notre Dame Cathedral

Notre Dame Cathedral is a beautiful Gothic cathedral that was built in the 12th century. It is one of the most famous churches in the world, and it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the largest and most famous museums in the world. It houses a vast collection of art and antiquities, including the Mona Lisa.

Total Tour Cost : 1,70,000 (For 6days/5nights)

Tour of Maldives :

Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical archipelago in the Indian Ocean. It is known for its white-sand beaches, crystal-clear lagoons, and stunning underwater scenery.

Male

Male is the capital of the Maldives. It is a small island with a population of over 150,000 people. Male is home to the Velana International Airport, the main shopping district, and the Grand Friday Mosque.

Water Sports

The Maldives is a great place to go for water sports. You can go snorkeling, diving, surfing, sailing, and more. There are also many resorts that offer water sports packages.

Total Tour Cost : 80,000 (For 4nights/5days)
